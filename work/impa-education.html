IMPA Education

An engaging marketing site for this education initiative.

The International Marine Purchasing Association (IMPA) required a simple brand along with a marketing website to detail their education courses and gather registrations.

We delivered a fun, informative site for prospective students, as well as a simple editorial experience for the course creators to update information, publish blogs and administer applications.
